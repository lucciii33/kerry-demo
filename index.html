Authority & Credentials

The Research Is the Foundation.
The Practice Is the Proof.

Dr. Kerry Mitchell Brown is CEO of kmb Consultancy and one of the country's leading authorities on organizational transformation, Black women's leadership, and the structural dynamics that shape advancement and culture.

For more than 30 years she has partnered with Fortune 100 companies, nonprofits, and government institutions. Her research spans two longitudinal studies and more than 600 Black women professionals — producing findings that challenge the conventional career playbook.

She holds a PhD in Organizations and Management and is Gestalt Organizational Systems Development certified. Author of Revolutionary Leadership: A Power Playbook for Black Women (Entrepreneur Books, a Forbes imprint).
